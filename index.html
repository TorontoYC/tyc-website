What We Do

Full-Service Marine Care

From a quick wash to a complete restoration — we handle it all so you can get back on the water.

Detailing

Interior and exterior detailing for cockpit, cabin, deck and hull. Deep cleaning, polishing, waxing and compound to keep your vessel looking its best.

Vinyl Wrap

Custom vinyl wrapping for hulls, accents and full colour changes using 3M, Avery Dennison and PPF Film. Custom designs, printed graphics and paint protection.

Fiberglass & Gel Coat Repair

From small scratches to major damage — our team handles fiberglass and gel coat repairs quickly and professionally.

Canvas & Upholstery

Custom boat tops, yacht interiors, canvas enclosures, custom framework and marine carpet. New builds and repairs.

SeaDek Flooring

Custom-cut SeaDek marine flooring — non-slip, UV-resistant and comfortable underfoot. Wide range of colours and trim options.

Bottom Paint

Professional hull prep and bottom paint application using antifouling paint and Interprotect 2000E to protect against marine growth.

Winterization

Shrink wrap, custom protective structures and full winterization service to protect your investment through the off-season.

Spring Launch

Shrink wrap removal and spring preparation to get your vessel water-ready when the season starts.
