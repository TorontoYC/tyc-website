Our Services

What We Offer

Comprehensive marine care from bow to stern. Detailing, repair, seasonal services and more — all backed by over 9 years of experience.

Detailing

Interior Detailing — Cockpit

Complete wash down of all exterior surfaces including gelcoat, stainless steel, rub rail, glass, fibreglass, and aluminium. Teak wood surfaces polished upon request.

Interior Detailing — Cabin

Thorough vacuum of all interior surfaces. Wash and clean all interior upholstery, wipe down of exposed surfaces including washrooms, bedrooms, kitchen and cabin areas.

Deck & Hull Bath

Intense wash down and deep scrub of all exposed surfaces to remove dirt, grime, bird droppings and oxidation. We go the extra mile to make sure your boat always looks inviting.

Polishing, Waxing & Compound

Full service detailing with complete wash, hand dry, high-speed buffer wax application on all fiberglass, and compounding to remove medium to heavy oxidation. All stainless steel cleaned and polished.

Ask us about our weekly and bi-weekly maintenance programs.

Repairs

Fiberglass & Gel Coat Repairs

From small scratches to major damage — count on our experienced team to fix any fiberglass issue quickly and efficiently.

Custom Canvas, Awnings & Upholstery

Boat tops, yacht interiors, custom canvas, custom framework, canvas extensions, full enclosures and repairs. Marine carpet, synthetic flooring and all related items.

Propeller & Lower Unit Repairs

Repair or replacement for lower unit damage. We work with aluminium, stainless steel, mercalloy and bronze.

Synthetic Teak Decking

Custom hand-made synthetic teak deck flooring. Choose from a variety of colour and trim options for a beautiful, durable finish.

Seasonal Services

Bottom Antifouling Paint

Professional application of antifouling paint to protect your hull from marine growth. Proper prep work and quality products.

Winterization & Shrink Wrap

Complete winterization service including shrink wrapping and building custom protective structures.

Spring Launch

Shrink wrap removal and full spring preparation to get your vessel ready for the water.

Need a Service Not Listed?

We accommodate vessels of all sizes. Give us a call.
