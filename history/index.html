About Us

Our Story

Toronto Yacht Club is a Toronto-based marine detailing and repair company proudly servicing the Greater Toronto Area and surrounding regions. With four locations and over 20,000 sq/ft of workspace, we are fully equipped to accommodate and service vessels of all sizes.

Our facilities are located at Krates Marina in Keswick, Driftwood Cove Marine Resort in Port Severn, Grand Harbour Marina in Brechin, and Downtown Toronto at the Harbourfront — allowing us to provide convenient access and professional care for boat owners across the region.

Our passion for the boating industry and the outdoors has helped us build strong, hands-on relationships with our customers and their vessels. Our mission is simple: to meet and exceed the needs of every customer we serve. We take pride in delivering the highest level of workmanship, attention to detail, and reliable service.

Our goal is to help keep your vessel in pristine condition and performing at its best for years to come. No vessel is too small and no vessel is too large. We are committed to going the extra mile to ensure your expectations are not only met but exceeded.

— Javier Enrique, Founder
